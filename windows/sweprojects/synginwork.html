SWE Projects 3/3 - Syngin

Attached is a frontend demonstration of the work I did at Syngin Technologies this summer. While I could not film any code (for obvious privacy reasons), I could film the features which utilized it. The new database I set up had to be connected to Salesforce so that our transportation, IT, and sales teams could view data on vehicle deliveries on a case-by-case basis. The video shown demonstrates my Salesforce LWC querying and pulling information from our new Azure Blob Storage database, to generate a list of photos related to the case for our teams to view, copy, and download. The PDF shown is the new proof-of delivery design I created, which also pulls pick-up and delivery photos, as well as a diagram of damages to the vehicle, and keys if provided.
